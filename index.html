Financially Aware Scholars

Educating diverse communities on financial literacy

Mission Statement

"Financially Aware Scholars" is dedicated to empowering people—especially youth—with essential financial knowledge to make informed decisions about money management, savings, investments, and financial independence.

Core Values

  1. Education First – Our primary goal is to provide accessible and engaging financial literacy education.
  2. Integrity – We are committed to honesty, transparency, and ethical practices in all that we do.
  3. Collaboration – We work together to maximize our impact.
  4. Innovation – We embrace creative solutions to make financial education engaging and effective.
  5. Inclusivity – We strive to reach diverse audiences and create a welcoming environment for all.

Roles

Each member has a critical role in our success. Below are the positions and their general responsibilities:

  • President/Founder: Oversees the nonprofit’s vision, growth, and strategic planning
  • Website Developer: Actively creates and enhances aspects of the website representing FAS
  • Researcher: Finds the information and organizes it for different things like posts, workshops, etc.
  • Content Planner: Generates ideas, details for the ideas, and how they should be executed for different content
  • Blogger: Creates blog ideas & outlines for them which are then posted on "Medium"
  • Graphic Designer: Designs posts, presentations, etc. fitting the themes and color schemes
